Shawn Melanson
Head Instructor & Co-Founder

Shawn's journey into Jiu Jitsu began at an early age, learning techniques from his older cousins. In 2011 he began training full-time in MMA, earning a 3–1 record and becoming the #2 ranked Featherweight in New England. By 2014, he switched his focus solely to Jiu Jitsu, winning multiple super fights and competitions. Today Shawn is one of the top black belts in the region, a featured instructor on BJJ Fanatics, and one of the most followed jiu jitsu athletes on the internet with over 200,000 followers.
